The approach

Flamboyant, but disciplined.

The flamingo is the whole idea: striking on the outside, exact underneath. Stillness charged with imminent motion.

01

You see everything

A private client portal shows what's done, what's next, and what's waiting on you. No chasing, no wondering where it stands.

02

One person, accountable

Founder-run. The person who scopes it is the person who builds it and answers the email. No handoffs into a void.

03

Kept, not abandoned

Sites and systems are maintained after launch — a live support layer, not a delivery and a goodbye.

3×

A Baltimore business that sources from Baltimore.

When the work calls for a hand we don't keep in-house — photo, print, illustration — it goes to a vetted local first. Money spent with a local business recirculates roughly three times more in the community than with a chain. The cloud plumbing has no local version; everything that does, we buy here.
